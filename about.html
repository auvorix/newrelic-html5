About Orbit Tech

Engineering-led company founded in Dublin in 2019. We build the infrastructure and software that keeps our clients ahead.

Our Story

Orbit Tech was founded in 2019 by Liam Farrell and Aoife Nolan, two senior engineers who had spent a decade building infrastructure at scale for large financial institutions. They left to start something smaller, faster, and more focused: a firm that brings enterprise-grade engineering discipline to ambitious companies that aren't enterprises yet.

We started with three clients and four engineers. Today we have a team of 28 across Dublin and Lisbon, and we've delivered over 180 projects across cloud infrastructure, custom software, and DevOps transformation.

We don't take on more than we can staff well. Every client has a named lead engineer who is accountable for delivery quality. That approach has given us a net promoter score of 74 and a client retention rate above 88%.
